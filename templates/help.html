This site was built as an experiment in using the SoundCloud public API along with Flask. The source code can be found on GitHub.


What is it?

By creating an account, you will be able follow the likes of different SoundCloud users such as your friends, music curators, and favorite artists. Over time, the site will monitor their likes to generate a stream of new songs you know you'll love. It makes it easier to follow the favorite songs of many users without having to manually monitor each user.

How is a stream generated?

SoundCloud does not provide timestamps of when songs have been liked by its users. When you start following a new user, the site will check to see if that user is already being tracked. If so, you're in luck: a history with timestamps will have already been created for them, and their likes will fit seamlessly into your stream. If not, no worries, that user's most recent likes will be added to the top of your stream and tracking will begin. After a few days, your stream will accurately reflect the likes of all the users you follow.


How real-time is it?

That depends. A background process will be collecting information on all the tracked users. The frequency of updates will be subject to the number of users to track and hosting service chosen.
